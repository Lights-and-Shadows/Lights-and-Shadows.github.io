Jamie Roeger

Game Designer / Artist

Hello there!

I'm Jamie, a gaming enthusiast and 3D modeling hobbyist, who wishes to ply his newfound technical chops into my own gaming content instead of always playing them. Not long after I got into gaming back in the early 2000's with the original Xbox console, I started wanting to create my own experiences. It was not until I started taking web design courses in middle school where I started building the foundation that would lead to where I am now. Over the past 15 years, I have studied various programming languages which include: Visual Basic, C / C++ / C#, Java / Javascript, and Blitz3D.

In addition to these, I have also been using Blender for six years, making three-dimensional models as a hobbyist as well as for experimental renderings using the LuxRender (now LuxCoreRender) rendering engine. I also have experience with Autodesk Maya and Rhinoceros 5.

I am currently studying for my Associate's in Animation and Graphic Game Programming.
