AstroCell

Multiplayer Puzzle Survival -- In Space

What is AstroCell?

AstroCell is my college capstone project for the 2020 / 2021 term, made in conjunction with four other classmates. The game is a multiplayer-focused puzzle and survival title where you play as either prisoners are guards, navigating inside an intergalactic prison. As a prisoner, you must work with your fellow cellmates in escaping the facility, while guards patrol the prison in attempts to catch prisoners with contraband items.

My most important contribution to this project was the creation -- start to finish -- of the player models, specifically the prisoner and guard models. I designed, modeled, textured, and animated both of them over the course of about two and a half months. I also helped model some of the map assets and also was responsible for the last-minute retexturing of the walls and floors. I also worked on the shield generator texture.

Another big contribution that I provided was the creation of the skybox asset. I had access to a nebula generator inside of Blender that produces high-quality renders of various nebula effects, in any color scheme you can dream up. I opted for a predominantly purple scheme, to contrast with the blues and oranges and grays present in the rest of the models.
