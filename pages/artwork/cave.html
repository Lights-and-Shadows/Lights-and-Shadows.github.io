Cave System

A Throwback Render

What is Cave System?

This is a revisitation of one of the very first Blender 3D tutorials that I ever followed, back in 2014. It is an image of a fantasy-stylized cave, complete with a medium-sized blue gemstone and various mushrooms littering the ground. At the mouth of the cave glares the sun, which incorporates what is known as complementary colors to create a balance.
